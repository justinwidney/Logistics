Health and Safety

Our health and safety programs are designed to help prevent workplace injuries and save lives. We're proud of our record. your health and safety initiatives and accomplishments include the following:

in-house training centre for orientation / regulatory training

regular health and safety meetings

extensive driver training program including classroom and on-board training

an on-staff recruiting and training manager

a certified forklift operations instructor

well-trained drivers and warehouse employees

shared knowledge between our business units

a comprehensive Safety Incentive Program that recognizes driving and warehouse employees who consistently excel in committing themselves to the Company's principles of safety.

Our certifications:

Certificate of Recognition

COR® is an occupational safety and health accreditation program that verifies a fully implemented safety and health management system which meets national standards. The objectives of COR® are to provide industry employers with effective tools to develop, implement, assess, and promote continual improvement of their safety and health management system to prevent or mitigate incidents and injuries as well as their associated human and financial costs.
