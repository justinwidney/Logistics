We Offer

Dedicated Delivery Services

Our competitive edge lies with our companies: each one specializing in a different market segment.

Dedicated delivery services allow companies to completely outsource their transportation needs, while maintaining benefits as if the client company had its own internal transportation / delivery services.

The Landtran Group provides dedicated delivery services to companies that require frequent and regularly-scheduled transportation services. To complement our dedicated delivery services, we also offer warehousing and distribution services. This arm of the Landtran Group is highly experienced and provides timely, reliable service to this specialized segment of the market across western Canada.
